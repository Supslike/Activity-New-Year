My Holiday Events

December 16th 2023

As we gathered on the 16th for the Frosty Mug Festival, laughter echoed through the air as we crafted and decorated our own frosty mugs. The hot chocolate tasting competition brought out the inner connoisseur in each of us, with unique blends and toppings making it a truly cozy start to the holiday celebrations.

December 24th 2023

On the eve of Christmas, we delved into the Marshmallow Masterclass. The kitchen became a sugary laboratory where we experimented with flavors, shapes, and sizes of marshmallows. The highlight was the bonfire where we toasted our creations, sharing stories under the starlit sky.

January 1st 2023

The year began with comfort and camaraderie at the Pajama Pancake Party. Donned in our favorite sleepwear, we shared tales of the previous night's celebrations and indulged in a variety of pancakes with a myriad of toppings. The perfect start to a brand new year filled with warmth and connection.

December 20th 2023

The Gingerbread House Marathon proved to be an architectural adventure. Our living rooms transformed into bustling workshops as friends and family unleashed their creativity. The sweet aroma of gingerbread filled the air, and the competition was fierce, with gingerbread structures ranging from candy-cane castles to log cabin masterpieces.
